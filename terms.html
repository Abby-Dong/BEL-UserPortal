Program Overview

The BEL (Business Exclusive Leader) Platform is an invitation-only partner program operated by IoTMart, designed to reward qualified business partners, system integrators, and industry professionals who promote IoTMart products and services. This program provides exclusive access to promotional tools, marketing resources, and business development opportunities to help partners grow their business while expanding IoTMart's market reach.

Eligibility and Invitation

Participation in the BEL Platform is by invitation only. IoTMart reserves the right to invite qualified candidates based on their industry expertise, business credentials, market influence, and alignment with IoTMart's business objectives. Invitations may be extended to system integrators, distributors, consultants, industry influencers, and other business professionals who demonstrate the ability to effectively promote IoTMart products and services.

Partner Levels and Benefits

Partners are classified into different levels based on their performance, engagement, and sales contribution. Each level offers different benefits and privileges. Progression through levels is determined by accumulated performance metrics and business development activities.

Level Requirements Benefits
Builder Level Entry level - No minimum requirements
  • Basic marketing materials
  • Standard support
Enabler Level Minimum 20 performance points
  • Enhanced marketing materials
  • Priority support
  • Quarterly business reviews
Explorer Level Minimum 35 performance points
  • Premium marketing materials
  • Dedicated account manager
  • Early product access
Leader Level Minimum 50 performance points
  • Custom marketing materials
  • Co-marketing opportunities
  • Strategic planning sessions

Payment Terms and Schedule

Partner benefits and rewards are processed monthly according to the following schedule: Benefit assessments are completed on the 5th of each month based on your performance points and partner level. Rewards and benefits are delivered according to your current level privileges. Benefits include marketing materials, priority support, and account management services as outlined in the partner level requirements above.

Referral Tracking and Attribution

IoTMart uses advanced tracking technology to ensure accurate attribution of referrals. Each partner receives a unique referral link and referral ID. Referral tracking cookies are valid for 30 days from the initial click. Multiple touchpoints from the same customer within the cookie period will be attributed to the original referrer. Partners are responsible for ensuring their referral materials comply with applicable laws and regulations.

Marketing Guidelines and Compliance

Partners must adhere to IoTMart's marketing guidelines when promoting products and services. All marketing materials and communications must be professional, accurate, and compliant with applicable laws and regulations. Partners may not engage in spam, misleading advertising, trademark infringement, or any activities that could harm IoTMart's reputation. Violation of marketing guidelines may result in benefit suspension and program termination.

Liability & Indemnification

IoTMart's liability under this program is limited to the provision of agreed partner benefits and services. IoTMart is not liable for indirect, special, incidental, or consequential damages. Partners participate in the program at their own risk and are responsible for their own business decisions and activities. The total liability of IoTMart shall not exceed the value of benefits provided to the partner in the preceding 12 months.
